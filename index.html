About Me

I am a first-year B.Tech CSE student at Karunya Institute of Technology and Sciences with a strong interest in building intelligent, real-world systems. My work focuses on combining Artificial Intelligence, IoT, and embedded systems to solve practical challenges. I have developed projects in areas such as healthcare monitoring, smart irrigation, campus safety, and accessibility solutions. These experiences have helped me understand how data, hardware, and software interact in real-time environments.

I enjoy designing systems end-to-end, from sensor integration and data processing to user-friendly interfaces. My technical skills include Python, C, React, MongoDB, and core web technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I am particularly interested in machine learning applications that enhance automation and decision-making. I focus on building solutions that are efficient, scalable, and easy to use.

I actively explore new technologies and continuously improve my development and problem-solving skills. My goal is to create impactful systems that bridge the gap between intelligent software and the physical world.
